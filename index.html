Head Teacher

As the Headteacher of Highfield Schools, it is my pleasure to welcome you to our school’s website. At our school, we pride ourselves on maintaining a reasonable number of enrolment. This allows our dedicated staff to provide personalized attention to each pupil, ensuring that every child is known, valued, and supported in their learning journey. We believe that our smaller class sizes offer a unique advantage, allowing teachers to connect with pupils on an individual level. This personalized approach enables us to understand each pupil’s strengths, challenges, and interests, tailoring our teaching methods to help them reach their full potential. In addition, we aim to create a safe, secure and sustainable learning environment where pupils feel valued and happy. Our pupils are encouraged, praised and taught to be responsible citizens, and take an active role in their community. We instill in them the importance of moral values such as honesty, respect, reliable, faithfully transparent, and personal responsibility. We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our programs, values, and the enriching experiences we offer. Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to welcoming you to our school community.

Mrs. Getrude Mutanda
Head Teacher

School Activities

SICK BAY

The school runs a sickbay, which takes care of minor ailments for pupils. Major cases are referred to the parents. Complicated cases are referred to hospital via parent/guardian.

AGRICULTURE

Our agriculture program provides students with hands-on learning experiences in gardening, crop cultivation, and animal care. Through this program, students gain practical skills in food production and an understanding of environmental stewardship. By working in our school gardens, students learn about responsibility, teamwork, and the value of hard work.

SPORTS ACTIVITIES

Through sports, students learn the importance of discipline, perseverance, and fair play, while also enjoying the health benefits of physical activity. At Highfield, we believe that sports not only enhance physical fitness but also inspire leadership, confidence, and a strong sense of community.

EDUCATIONAL TRIPS

we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom. Our educational trips offer students the opportunity to explore new environments, experience different cultures, and gain practical knowledge

MUSIC DANCE AND DRAMA

we celebrate the rich talents of our students through our vibrant Music, Dance, and Drama (MDD) program. MDD plays an essential role in our curriculum, encouraging students to express themselves creatively, build confidence, and explore diverse cultures.

CHURCH

Highfield Primary School Gayaza is rooted in the values and beliefs of the Seventh-day Adventist Church. Our commitment to faith shapes our approach to education, emphasizing character development, compassion, and a strong moral foundation. Through regular worship, Bible study, and spiritual guidance, we encourage students to deepen their relationship with God and to live out Christian principles in their daily lives.

Our Aims, Objectives and Values

Our aim in partnership with parents, we seek to ensure all students:

• Receive an excellent and rich education, achieving to the best of their ability.

• Are happy, healthy, confident, independent and motivated learners.

• Have a strong foundation of values to use to make informed choices in a local and global community.

• To be able to fit into a society that awaits them in Uganda and on their travels abroad.

• Develop a love for learning and are motivated by education.

• Are developed as individuals and that their diverse strengths, cultural backgrounds, gifts and aspirations are celebrated

Objectives

1. To provide a learning experience of a harmonious development of a child through mental, physical, social and spiritual development.

2. To provide learning experience that will develop a pupils into strong, flexible, creative and independent minded human being.

3. Develop the mental discipline required for academic success.

4. To provide education that leads to the discovery and use of each pupil’s individual talent.
